Skill Squatting Attacks on Amazon Alexa

September 2022 – November 2022

Developed skills using Alexa Skill Developer Console and AWS Lambdas to analyze the speech recognition errors made by Alexa. Such errors can be used by attackers to route users to malicious third-party software.

Data Analysis of JSON files to categorize the words and identify patterns in errors was performed using Python.

AWS Dynamo DB was utilized for mapping actual to interpreted words. The input samples were obtained using NSP corpus.

Analysis resulted in the identification of words with a 0% accuracy rate and the range of words that a given input word may be misinterpreted as by Alexa and their respective probabilities.
